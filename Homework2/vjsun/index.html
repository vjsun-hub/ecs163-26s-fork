Pokémon Type Dashboard

Pokémon can have up to two elemental typings: a primary type and an optional secondary type. Each Pokémon also has six core battle stats: HP, Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed. Legendary Pokémon are rare and exceptionally powerful Pokémon that are typically much harder to catch. This dashboard explores Pokémon primary types through type distribution, average battle stat profiles, and legendary typing patterns.
